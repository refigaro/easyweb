Got questions? We have answers.

We solve the most common questions so you can make the best decision.

Do I have to pay a monthly fee for my website?
Not for the website design. Our packages are a one-time payment and you own the site 100%. However, like any website, you will need yearly Hosting and a Domain (.com). We can guide you to get the best and most affordable ones.
Can I edit texts and images myself later?
Absolutely! Our sites are built on WordPress with a super intuitive dashboard. We'll provide a short video tutorial so you can change texts, photos, or add products without needing to code.
What if I don't like the initial design?
We won't move to the coding stage until you are 100% happy. First, we present visual mockups for you to review. All our plans include 2 rounds of design revisions to refine colors, typography, and structure.
Do you include copywriting or photos?
Ideally, you provide the text about your business (about us, services) and your own photos. If you don't have photos, we can include high-quality, royalty-free stock images at no extra cost.
